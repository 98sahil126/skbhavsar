Who We Are

S K Bhavsar and co was set-up in the year 2017 by Shivam Bhavsar. Shivam Bhavsar started its journey in the field of accounting & auditing services in 2017. We are prominent Chartered Accountants Firm in India, providing services in the field of auditing, Direct & indirect taxation, company formation in India, starting business in India, corporate compliances, taxation of expatriates etc. We have a client centric approach with a motive to serve client needs. We endeavor for a vibrant and long-lasting client relationship.

How We Are Different!!

SKB is a team of prominent Chartered Accountants & business consultants in India. The organization is a combination of professionally qualified & experienced persons who are dedicated to add value & optimize the benefits accruing to clients.
